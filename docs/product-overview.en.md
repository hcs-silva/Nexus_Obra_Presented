# Product Overview

## Positioning

Nexus Obra is a construction management platform that connects field operations, financial control, and execution tracking in one workflow.

## Problems it solves

- Fragmentation between planning, budgeting, and daily records.
- Lack of visibility into cost and schedule deviations.
- Late decisions caused by scattered operational data.

## Value proposition

- Daily operations with cost and progress context.
- Budgeting and revisions with strong control discipline.
- Team workflows with traceable history.

## Core modules

- Operational dashboard and alerts.
- Project and phase management.
- Budgeting and BOQ import.
- Time tracking and attendance.
- Planning and task control.

## Target audience

- Owners and technical leadership.
- Project managers and site coordinators.
- Field operations teams.
