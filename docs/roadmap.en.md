# Roadmap (Balanced Product + Engineering)

## Short-term horizon

- UX polish for budgeting and daily control workflows.
- Usability improvements in planning and workforce modules.
- Performance optimization for high-density lists and panels.

## Mid-term horizon

- Operational analytics focused on cost, productivity, and risk.
- Observability evolution for proactive anomaly detection.
- Expanded test coverage for multi-tenant flows.

## Long-term horizon

- Organizational scaling with more granular tenant governance.
- Construction lifecycle automation and decision support.
- Higher compliance maturity and advanced audit trails.

## Continuous priorities

- Release confidence.
- Security by default.
- Data clarity for field decision-making.
